Here’s a detailed write-up for a University Hostel Booking System tailored for GCTU (Ghana Communication Technology University): University Hostel Booking System for GCTU Introduction Ghana Communication Technology University (GCTU) accommodates students from diverse locations. Managing hostel facilities efficiently is critical to ensuring students have a seamless and comfortable stay on campus. The proposed GCTU Hostel Booking System is designed to streamline the process of room allocation, payment, and record management for both students and hostel administrators. System Objectives Ease of Booking: Simplify the process of reserving hostel rooms through an online platform accessible 24/7. Transparency: Provide real-time updates on room availability, pricing, and rules. Payment Integration: Allow secure payment options through mobile money, credit cards, and bank transfers. Data Management: Maintain accurate records of students, room allocations, and payment histories. Communication: Enable notifications for booking confirmations, reminders for payment deadlines, and updates on hostel policies.

Benefits of the System For Students: Eliminates the hassle of manual booking processes. Ensures fair allocation based on availability. Provides transparency in pricing and rules. For Administrators: Reduces administrative workload by automating routine tasks. Improves accuracy in record-keeping and payment tracking. Facilitates better resource planning through data analytics. For GCTU: Enhances the university’s reputation by offering modern and efficient services. Ensures optimal utilization of hostel facilities. Generates detailed insights for decision-making
